### 2. HR Attrition Analysis
Tools: Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn
Link: https://github.com/joshinikitawork-cmd/HR-Attrition-Analysis

Business problem: Identify key factors driving 16.12% employee 
attrition rate across departments and salary bands.

Key findings:
- R&D department has highest attrition
- Age group 26-35 leaves most frequently
- Overtime workers leave at higher rates
- Low income strongly predicts attrition
